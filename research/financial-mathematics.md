# Financial Mathematics

Financial mathematics provides the foundational quantitative framework used to model markets, price assets, and manage risk. In quantum computing, these classical formulations are translated into quantum circuits to achieve computational speedups.

#### Key Mathematical Concepts

* Stochastic Calculus: Modeling asset price movements over time using geometric Brownian motion (GBM).
* Time Value of Money (TVM): Calculating the present and future values of cash flows using discount factors.
* Probability Distributions: Utilizing normal and log-normal distributions to model market returns and volatility.

#### Quantum Application

Quantum Amplitude Estimation (QAE) can be applied to financial mathematics to price complex derivative products and calculate Value-at-Risk (VaR) significantly faster than classical Monte Carlo simulations.
